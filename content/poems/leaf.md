孤<span style="color: #e74c3c">叶</span>枝壳垂，
夕珠<span style="color: #e74c3c">红</span>缘坠。
朦<span style="color: #3498db">雾</span>残魂萧，
手瘦时噬<span style="color: #3498db">泪</span>。

<!--more-->

This poem is about a maple leaf, supposedly always red.
